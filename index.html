The Seven Sisters

Myself infront of The Seven Sisters

The Seven Sisters is a mountain range in Alstahaug municipality in Nordland. The seven mountain peaks are from the south Breitind (also called Stortind), Kvasstind, Tvillingan (twins), Skjæringen, Grytfoten and Botnkrona.

Botnkrona is the highest of the mountain peaks with its 1072 metres.

In the south you will find Alstahaug, where the famous poet-priest Petter Dass worked. The small town of Sandnessjøen is located in the very north of the mountain range.

I myself come from a small place called Søroffersøy, which is behind Breitind. Breitind is also the only one of the mountain peaks that I have climbed.
